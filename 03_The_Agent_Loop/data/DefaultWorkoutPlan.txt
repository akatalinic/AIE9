Single Full-Body Workout Plan

This workout plan is designed for general fitness.

Workout Frequency
3 times per week (e.g., Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

Warm-Up (5–10 minutes)
• Arm circles – 30 seconds
• Bodyweight squats – 15 reps
• Hip rotations – 30 seconds
• Light jogging or marching in place – 2 minutes

Main Workout
• Push-ups – 3 sets x 10–15 reps
• Bodyweight squats – 3 sets x 15 reps
• Plank – 3 sets x 30–45 seconds
• Dumbbell rows or resistance band rows – 3 sets x 12 reps
• Walking lunges – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg

Cool Down (5 minutes)
• Hamstring stretch – 30 seconds per leg
• Chest and shoulder stretch – 30 seconds
• Deep breathing – 1 minute

Notes
Adjust repetitions based on your fitness level. Stop if you feel pain or dizziness. This plan is
intended for general wellness and not as medical advice.
